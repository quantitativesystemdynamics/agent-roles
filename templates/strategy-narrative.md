# Strategy Narrative Template

## Purpose
Document strategic direction in a clear, compelling narrative format for stakeholder communication.

---

## Structure

### Vision Statement
[One paragraph describing the aspirational future state]

### Strategic Bets
[2-3 major strategic initiatives with rationale]

| Bet | Rationale | Time Horizon |
|-----|-----------|--------------|
| [Initiative] | [Why this matters] | [Timeline] |

### Key Metrics
[3-5 metrics that indicate strategic progress]

| Metric | Current | Target | Timeline |
|--------|---------|--------|----------|
| [Name] | [Value] | [Goal] | [Date] |

### Risks and Mitigations
[Major risks to strategy execution]

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| [Risk] | [H/M/L] | [H/M/L] | [Action] |

### Resource Requirements
[Key resources needed to execute]

---

## Usage Notes
- Update quarterly or when strategy changes significantly
- Keep language accessible to all stakeholders
- Focus on "why" more than "what"
- Include both short-term and long-term elements
